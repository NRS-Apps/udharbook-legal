Privacy Policy – UdharBook

Last updated: February 2026

UdharBook (“we”, “our”, “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the UdharBook mobile application.

By using UdharBook, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

  • Phone number (for authentication)
  • Name (if provided by you)
  • Profile details you enter manually

This information is used only to provide app functionality.

b) Contacts Access (Optional)

If you grant contact permission, UdharBook allows you to pick contacts to quickly add borrowers or lenders.

  • We do NOT upload or store your contacts on our servers.
  • Contacts are accessed only locally on your device.
  • Permission is optional and can be denied.

c) Financial Data You Enter

  • Borrower or lender names
  • Amounts
  • Dates
  • Notes related to your records

This data is used only to manage your udhar (credit/debit) entries.

d) Device & Usage Information

We may collect limited technical data such as:

  • App version
  • Device type
  • Crash logs
  • Anonymous usage events

This helps improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core app features
  • Authenticate users
  • Store and display your udhar records
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Send important app-related notifications (if enabled)

We do NOT sell your data.

3. Notifications

If you allow notifications, UdharBook may send reminders or updates related to your records. You can disable notifications anytime from your device settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely.
  • We use standard security practices to protect your information.
  • No system is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

UdharBook may use trusted third-party services for:

  • Authentication
  • Analytics
  • Push notifications

These services may collect limited technical data as required to function.

6. Your Choices

  • Deny contact permissions
  • Disable notifications
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us for data-related requests

7. Children’s Privacy

UdharBook is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
